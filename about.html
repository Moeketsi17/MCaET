Radio Installation and maintainance is our speciality

Moletsi Communication and Electrical Technologies (PTY)LTD was estblished in 2017 by Madira A. Phetla who acquired most of his experience in electronic communications from various companies such as Becker Mining SA, Motorola SA and more.

We specialize in two-way radio installation and supply to commercial organiszations, government, public safety and mining industries. We support both analogue and digital communication systems. We provide the best underground and/or tunnel communication systems where having a simple yet relaible communication system is paramount.

Our mission

Our mission is to offer the best services to our clients. We understand that communication is important in our clients line of business so we are available 24/7 even on ‘mission critical’ areas like mining.

Our vission

Our vision is to be the most reliable and most trustworthy company when it comes to radio communications solution in and around South Africa and expanding to manufacturing of electronic communication devices focusing mainly towards developing young african designers and developers
