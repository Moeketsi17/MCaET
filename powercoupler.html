Power Coupler VHF
Automatic Line Length Compensations

Frequency: 20 to 175Mhz

The VPC is VHF Power Coupler that is designed to allow for thecoupling of DC power into the leaky feeder cable to power the bi_directional amplifiers in the vicinity.

The VPC can be configured to couple power in a single directionor both directions by fitting a jumper.

  • RF passes through with minimal loss
  • Well matched impedances
  • DC poewer can be supplied to Down link or Uplink as well as Downlink
  • Robust enclosurewith Ip66 rating
